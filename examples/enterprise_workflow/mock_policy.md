# Mock Outreach Policy

- Do not claim direct referral unless a contact agreed.
- Do not invent work authorization details.
- Do not exaggerate project metrics.
- Do not send emails automatically without explicit approval.
- Do not disclose sensitive personal data unless required.
- Do not create contacts that are missing from `mock_contacts.json`.
- Allow draft-only outreach and read-only matching actions.
- Allow CRM notes and status updates only when risk is low.
- Route referral requests and email sending into approval tasks.
- Deny destructive actions such as candidate deletion.
